About EZ Power

The Need

As many have already, powerbanks make it so you have power with you anywhere you go. These powerbanks usually can charge a cellphone. How much the cellphone actually charges is dependent on how big the battery is. Our powerbanks are bigger from the average ones, as they run off of bigger batteries. Our smallest tops out at 12 volts. This will charge your cellphone, and other devices, multiple times each.


Since we all have electronics, we want to be able to charge them whenever and wherever necessary. In activities like camping or ATV riding, for example, we can see how being able to charge an electronic whenever is hard. Our products are portable and can travel with you wherever you go, making it possible for you to have power whenever and wherever. Being in containers like Army Ammo boxes, they are well protected and strong enough to take a beating and still keep going. The containers also have some space to place smaller electronics to be protected. In our biggest generators, it can store the electronics you are trying to power. You can also see how convienent these can be in the event of a power outage, or emergency in which you need power. As we have seen in previous emergencies, like Hurricane Katrina, people have needed power for various necessities, the simplest to charge a phone and keep in contact with loved ones. The generators can even be used to help large groups that are sheltered. So as you can see, these are very useful for everyday activities, recreation, and emergencies. All that said, it is always a good idea to have some backup power.

Building your own

Here you will find links to videos and a list of materials in order to make your own if you choose to do so. Keep in mind how expensive the very small powerbanks are when making as it will cost you a bit more to make one yourself.

    List of Materials needed (most can be found on Amazon.com)
  1. Army Ammo box, or some sort of container that is big enough to hold the 12 volt battery
  2. 12 Volt battery
  3. Electrical Wire
  4. Power switch
  5. Volt Meter/reader
  6. Plugs for charging. Can do both usb and car outlet, or just one you prefer.
